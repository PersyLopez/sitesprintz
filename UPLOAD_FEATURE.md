## How to Add Photos Like Facebook

You can now upload profile pictures, cover photos, and custom images from your phone or computer!

### **Access the Admin Panel:**
1. Go to: **http://localhost:3000/admin.html**
2. Enter token: `test-admin-token-12345`
3. Click **"Load"** to get your current site

### **Upload Images:**
1. In the **"Images"** section at the top
2. Click **"Choose File"** and select a photo from your phone/computer
3. Preview appears instantly
4. Click **"Upload"**
5. Copy the URL and paste it in your site content (hero image, profile photo, etc.)

### **Use Cases:**
- **Profile/Cover Photo:** Upload photos and use the URL in your hero or about sections
- **Product Photos:** Add images to your products/services
- **Custom Branding:** Upload your business logo, team photos, or office images

### **Example Usage:**
After uploading, you'll get a URL like `/uploads/1234567890-photo.jpg`

Use this URL in your site content:
- Hero image
- Profile photos  
- Product images
- Gallery photos
- Service photos
