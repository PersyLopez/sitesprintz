## üí° Key Insights

### **Why This Works**

**1. Multiple Revenue Streams**
- Not dependent on single product
- Cross-sell opportunities
- Bundle discounts encourage upgrades

**2. Standalone Products**
- Reach customers who don't need full platform
- Lower barrier to entry ($9-39/mo vs $45-100/mo)
- Each product attracts different audience

**3. Platform Bundle**
- Ultimate upsell target
- Locks in customers
- Higher LTV

### **Competitive Advantages**

- Native integrations (no third-party dependencies)
- Single dashboard for all tools
- Better pricing than buying separately
- Designed for small businesses
