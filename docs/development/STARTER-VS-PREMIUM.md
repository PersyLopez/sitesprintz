## Tier Details

### Starter Tier (Free Forever)

**What It Includes:**

1. **Professional Website**
   - Beautiful template with industry-specific design
   - Fully responsive and mobile-optimized
   - SEO-friendly structure
   - Fast loading speeds

2. **Content Display**
   - Services or products listing with pricing
   - About/story section
   - Team member profiles
   - Customer testimonials
   - FAQ section
   - Contact information with hours and location

3. **Order Submission via Email**
   - Customers can submit orders through forms
   - Business owner receives email notifications
   - Customer receives confirmation email
   - No dashboard (owner manages via email)

4. **Contact Features**
   - Contact form with validation
   - Click-to-call phone numbers
   - Click-to-email links
   - Google Maps integration (if address provided)

5. **Basic Customization**
   - Edit all content through simple forms
   - Upload custom images
   - Adjust colors and branding
   - Update pricing and services

**What It Doesn't Include:**

- ‚ùå No orders dashboard
- ‚ùå No calendar integration
- ‚ùå No payment processing
- ‚ùå No customer database
- ‚ùå No order status updates
- ‚ùå No analytics reporting

**Perfect For:**

- New businesses just getting online
- Businesses that prefer phone/email contact
- Simple service businesses
- Information/brochure sites
- Testing the platform

**The Challenge:**

Managing orders via email can become chaotic as you grow:
- Hard to track which orders are complete
- Easy to lose emails
- No central place to see all orders
- Manual customer communication
- No order history or analytics

**Upgrade Motivation:**

As businesses receive more orders, email management becomes overwhelming. Upgrading to Premium provides the dashboard that eliminates this chaos.

---

### Premium/Pro Tier

**Everything in Starter, PLUS:**

1. **Orders Dashboard**
   - View all orders in one place
   - Filter by status (pending, completed, cancelled)
   - Search orders by customer or date
   - Mark orders as complete
   - Add notes to orders

2. **Order Management**
   - Update order status
   - Track order history
   - Communicate with customers
   - Export order data
   - Set up order workflows

3. **Calendar Integration**
   - Embed Calendly, Acuity, or Google Calendar
   - Show real-time availability
   - Automated booking confirmation
   - Sync with your calendar
   - Prevent double-bookings

4. **Customer Management**
   - Customer database
   - Order history per customer
   - Contact information storage
   - Notes and tags
   - Repeat customer tracking

5. **Analytics & Reporting**
   - Order volume trends
   - Revenue tracking
   - Popular services/products
   - Customer insights
   - Performance metrics

6. **Enhanced Communication**
   - Email customers from dashboard
   - Order status updates
   - Automated confirmations
   - Customizable email templates

**Perfect For:**

- Growing businesses with regular orders
- Service businesses needing scheduling
- Businesses wanting better organization
- Teams managing orders together
- Businesses tracking metrics

**The Limitation:**

While Premium provides excellent order management, customers still pay on-site or via invoice. No online payment collection.

**Upgrade Motivation:**

Businesses wanting to accept payments online and reduce friction in the buying process should upgrade to Checkout tier.

---

## Decision Tree

### Should You Use Starter?

**YES, if you:**
- Are just starting out
- Get fewer than 10 orders/week
- Prefer phone/email contact
- Don't need online payments
- Want a simple, free solution

**Consider upgrading if:**
- Managing email orders is getting messy
- You're losing track of orders
- Customers want online booking
- You need order history
- You want to look more professional

### Should You Use Premium?

**YES, if you:**
- Get 10+ orders/week
- Need better organization
- Want calendar scheduling
- Need order tracking
- Want customer database
- Need basic analytics

**Consider upgrading to Checkout if:**
- Want to accept online payments
- Want to reduce payment friction
- Need automated billing
- Want higher conversion rates
- Need advanced financial reporting

### Should You Use Checkout?

**YES, if you:**
- Sell products online
- Want online payment collection
- Need subscription billing
- Process high order volume
- Want full e-commerce features

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I switch between tiers?

**A:** Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Your content is preserved.

### Q: What happens to my orders if I downgrade?

**A:** Order data is preserved, but you lose dashboard access. Orders go back to email-only.

### Q: Do I need technical skills to use Premium/Checkout?

**A:** No! If you can use Starter, you can use Premium/Checkout. The interface is just as simple.

### Q: How long does it take to upgrade?

**A:** Instant! Click upgrade, choose your plan, and you immediately get access to new features.

### Q: Can I try Premium before committing?

**A:** Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of Premium tier.
